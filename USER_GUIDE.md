# VideoFlow User Guide

Welcome to VideoFlow! This guide will help you understand how to use the app to create amazing short-form videos for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

## What is VideoFlow?

VideoFlow is your personal video creation assistant. It helps you organize video projects and uses AI to generate scripts tailored to your goals. Think of it as a project manager for your video content.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create a New Project

1. Click the **New Project** button in the top right corner
2. Fill in your project details:
   - **Project Title**: Give your video a name (e.g., "Summer Sale Promo")
   - **Platform**: Choose where you'll post (TikTok, Instagram Reels, or YouTube Shorts)
   - **Video Length**: Select 15, 30, or 60 seconds
   - **Purpose**: What type of video is this? (testimonial, educational, promotional, etc.)
   - **Tone**: How should it feel? (emotional, humorous, professional, etc.)
   - **Key Phrase** (optional): A specific phrase to include in your script
   - **Keyword** (optional): A focus word for your content
3. Click **Create Project**

### Step 2: Generate Your Script with AI

1. Click on your project card to open it
2. Click the **Generate with AI** button
3. Wait a few seconds while AI creates your personalized script
4. You'll receive:
   - A ready-to-use video script
   - An image prompt for creating visuals
   - A video prompt for B-roll or footage ideas

### Step 3: Copy Your Content

Use the copy buttons next to each section to easily paste your script or prompts into other tools.

### Step 4: Track Your Workflow Progress

Use the checklist to track your video creation journey:

1. **Hook Generated**: Check this when you've created your attention-grabbing opening
2. **Image Created**: Check this after generating or sourcing your images
3. **Video Generated**: Check this when your video footage is ready
4. **Audio Generated**: Check this after creating voiceover or music
5. **Editing Complete**: Check this when your final video is done

## Understanding the Dashboard

Your dashboard shows:
- **Total Projects**: How many video projects you've created
- **Scripts Generated**: How many AI scripts you've made
- **Your Projects**: All your video projects as cards

Each project card displays:
- Platform badge (TikTok, Reels, or Shorts)
- Video length
- Purpose and tone
- Workflow progress tracker
- Creation date

## Tips for Best Results

### Writing Good Key Phrases
- Use phrases that resonate with your audience
- Examples: "Transform your morning routine", "Save 50% today only"

### Choosing Keywords
- Pick words central to your message
- Examples: "wellness", "savings", "community"

### Platform Considerations
- **TikTok**: Works well with trendy, casual content
- **Instagram Reels**: Great for polished, lifestyle content
- **YouTube Shorts**: Good for educational or entertaining clips

## Recommended External Tools

After generating your script, you might use these tools (not included in VideoFlow):

- **Hook Ideas**: transitionalhooks.com for attention-grabbing openings
- **Images/Video**: AI image generators or stock footage sites
- **Voiceover**: ElevenLabs or similar voice generation tools
- **Editing**: CapCut for final video assembly

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does AI script generation take?**
A: Usually 5-15 seconds depending on the complexity.

**Q: Can I edit the generated script?**
A: Currently, scripts are generated fresh each time. Copy the script to your preferred text editor to make changes.

**Q: What if the AI script isn't quite right?**
A: Try adjusting your purpose, tone, or keywords and generate again. Different combinations produce different results.

**Q: Is my project data saved?**
A: Yes, your projects are saved automatically. They'll be there when you return.

## Need Help?

If you have questions or run into issues, the best approach is to:
1. Try refreshing the page
2. Make sure all required fields are filled in
3. Wait for loading indicators to complete before clicking again
